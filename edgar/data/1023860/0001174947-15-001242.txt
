<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>PRESS RELEASE</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>STEWARDSHIP FINANCIAL CORPORATION</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center">DECLARES CASH DIVIDEND</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: center"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/2 Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in"><B>Midland Park, NJ &ndash;
July 22, 2015</B> &ndash; The Board of Directors of Stewardship Financial Corporation (NASDAQ: SSFN), parent company of Atlantic
Stewardship Bank, has declared a $0.02 per share cash dividend. Common stockholders of record as of August 3, 2015 will be paid
the dividend on August 17, 2015.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/2 Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">In announcing the dividend,
Chairman Richard Culp stated, &ldquo;We are pleased to continue to show our commitment to our shareholders by recognizing them
with this dividend. This dividend represents the seventy first consecutive quarterly dividend since the Corporation began paying
quarterly dividends in 1998.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/2 Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Paul Van Ostenbridge, President
and Chief Executive Officer continued, &ldquo;The Board of Directors remains committed to providing value to our shareholders while
recognizing our obligation to maintain a strong capital position.&rdquo;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/2 Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">Stewardship Financial Corporation&rsquo;s
subsidiary, the Atlantic Stewardship Bank, has 12 banking offices in Midland Park, Hawthorne (2), Montville, North Haledon, Pequannock,
Ridgewood, Waldwick, Wayne (2), Westwood and Wyckoff, New Jersey. The bank is known for tithing 10% of its pre-tax profits to Christian
and local charities. To date, the Bank&rsquo;s total tithe donations total $8.2 million.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt/2 Arial, Helvetica, Sans-Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">We invite you to visit
our website at <U>www.asbnow.com</U> for additional information.</P>
