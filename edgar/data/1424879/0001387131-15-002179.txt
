<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On June 24, 2014, Moody
National REIT I, Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;) acquired fee simple title to a 127 room hotel property located in located in
Newark, California commonly known as the TownePlace Suites by Marriott Newark/Silicon Valley (the &ldquo;Silicon Valley Hotel&rdquo;)
for an aggregate purchase price of $10 million, excluding closing costs.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">On July 20, 2015, the Company
entered into an agreement with respect to the sale of the Silicon Valley Hotel to a third-party purchaser for an aggregate sales
price of $24.5 million.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">The closing of the sale
of the Silicon Valley Hotel is subject to substantial closing conditions. There is no guarantee that the Company will complete
the sale of the Silicon Valley Hotel on the terms described above, or at all.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-align: justify; text-indent: 0.5in">&nbsp;</P>
