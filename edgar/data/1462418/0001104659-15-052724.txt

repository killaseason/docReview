<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p align="center" style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-align:center;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Fintech Leader Acquires Specialty Risk Management and Insurance Services Firm</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">LUXEMBOURG</font></b>, July&nbsp;23, 2015 &#151; Altisource Portfolio Solutions S.A. (&#147;Altisource&#148;) (NASDAQ: ASPS), a leading provider of mortgage, financial and technology services for the real estate industry, today announced the acquisition of CastleLine Holdings, LLC (&#147;CastleLine&#148;), a specialty risk management and insurance services firm.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">CastleLine is the leading provider of Certified Loan Insurance products designed to protect mortgage market participants against losses caused by mortgage underwriting defects.&#160; The innovative Certified Loan Insurance Program is enabled by the CastleLine</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#153;</font>&#160;proprietary certification process.&#160; With the&#160; acquisition of CastleLine, Altisource strengthens its origination related offerings with products and services focused on mitigating risk in the origination, underwriting, purchase and securitization of residential mortgages and offers the opportunity to expand CastleLine&#146;s products and services into contiguous markets.&#160; CastleLine&#146;s leadership team will join Altisource.</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&#147;The acquisition of CastleLine aligns with our strategy to continue helping the mortgage banking industry safely and securely increase production while reducing costs and mitigating risks.&#160; Combined with our existing origination offerings through Altisource</font><font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;Origination Services and our access to the members of the Lenders One<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;and Wholesale One<font size="1" style="font-size:6.5pt;position:relative;top:-3.0pt;">&#174;</font>&#160;cooperatives, we add meaningful synergies to the existing CastleLine business model,&#148; said William B. Shepro, Chief Executive Officer of Altisource. &#147;The acquisition also brings with it a highly talented leadership team.&#160; I am excited to welcome the CastleLine team to the Altisource family.&#148;</p>
