<P align="left" style="font-size: 10pt"><FONT style="font-size: 9pt"><B>Item&nbsp;5.07 Submission of Matters to a Vote of Security Holders.</B>
</FONT>

<P align="left" style="font-size: 9pt">Mitcham Industries, Inc. (the &#147;Company&#148;) held its 2015 Annual Meeting of Shareholders (the &#147;Annual
Meeting&#148;) on July&nbsp;23, 2015 in Huntsville, Texas. At the Annual Meeting, shareholders were requested
to (1)&nbsp;elect six individuals to serve on the Board of Directors until the next annual meeting of
shareholders, each until their respective successors are duly elected and qualified; (2)&nbsp;approve,
on an advisory basis, Named Executive Officer compensation; and (3)&nbsp;ratify the selection by the
Audit Committee of the Board of Directors of Hein & Associates LLP as the Company&#146;s independent
registered public accounting firm for the fiscal year ending January&nbsp;31, 2016. Each proposal was
described in the Company&#146;s Notice of Annual Meeting and the Company&#146;s Definitive Proxy Statement on
Schedule&nbsp;14A, which were each filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on May&nbsp;29, 2015.
The following actions were taken by the Company&#146;s shareholders at the Annual Meeting with respect
to each of the proposals:
