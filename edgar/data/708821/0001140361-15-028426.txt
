<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: left; FONT-STYLE: italic; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">Appointment of Michael S. Bartusek as Vice President and Chief Financial Officer</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">On July 17, 2015, PAR Technology Corporation (the &#8220;Company&#8221;) appointed Michael S. Bartusek to serve as Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company. Mr. Bartusek joined the Company on July 20.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Bartusek, age 46, has over 24 years of extensive financial management and diverse business experience. Mr. Bartusek formerly served as the Chief Financial Officer and Corporate Treasurer at Sutherland Global Services, Inc. (&#8220;SGS&#8221;) a $900M business process outsourcer, from 2007 to October 2014, where he provided financial leadership across 15 countries and established a global finance shared services operation. At SGS, Mr. Bartusek was responsible for overseeing and transforming financial operations, as well as handling strategic acquisitions. Prior to SGS, Mr. Bartusek was Director of Finance for the North American operations at XEROX Global Services, Inc. from 2004 to 2007. Prior to joining XEROX Global Services, Mr. Bartusek held various financial positions at GE Capital and was a Senior Manager at PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP where he gained experience in the assurance and business advisory group working with large public companies as well as emerging technology organizations. Mr. Bartusek graduated from Edinboro University with a B.S. in Business Administration, is a Certified Public Accountant, and holds a Green Belt Six Sigma certification.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Bartusek will be compensated at an annual base salary of $240,000 and will participate in the Company&#8217;s Annual Incentive Compensation Plan (&#8220;AICP&#8221;) at the rate of 20% of his earned annual base salary based upon the Company&#8217;s performance against its Annual Operating Plan. The amount earned by Mr. Bartusek under the AICP will be pro-rated based on Mr. Bartusek&#8217;s hire date for 2015.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Bartusek&#8217;s benefits include participation in the Company&#8217;s Retirement Plan and insurance benefits, as well as reimbursement for reasonable relocation and living expenses. Mr. Bartusek will be granted a non-qualified stock option to purchase up to 60,000 shares of the Company&#8217;s common stock (&#8220;Option Shares&#8221;) in accordance with the Company&#8217;s 2005 Equity Incentive Plan at the next scheduled date for option grants of the Company&#8217;s Board of Directors. Vesting of the Option Shares will occur at the rate of 25% each year on the anniversary date of his appointment, with all Option Shares being vested on July 20, 2019.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">In the event of termination by the Company without cause prior to July 20, 2017, Mr. Bartusek would be entitled to six (6) months&#8217; severance. This payment would be subject to and conditioned upon execution of the Company&#8217;s standard general release of claims.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">The Company&#8217;s press release announcing Mr. Bartusek&#8217;s appointment is included as Exhibit 99.1.</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: center; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt; FONT-WEIGHT: bold">NAMES NEW CFO</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">New Hartford, NY. -- July 23, 2015 &#8211;&#160; PAR Technology Corporation (NYSE: PAR) announced today that Michael Bartusek has been appointed as the Company&#8217;s new Vice President and Chief Financial Officer, effective immediately.&#160; Mr. Bartusek is a key addition to the Company&#8217;s leadership team and his comprehensive experience in financial and operational management will complement the existing executive team as the Company continues to focus on executing its strategic initiatives to transform&#160;its business.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">Mr. Bartusek has over 24 years of extensive financial management and diverse business experience. Mr. Bartusek formerly served as the Chief Financial Officer and Corporate Treasurer at Sutherland Global Services, Inc. (&#8220;SGS&#8221;) where he provided financial leadership across 15 countries and established a global finance shared services operation.&#160; At SGS, Mr. Bartusek was responsible for overseeing and transforming financial operations, as well as handling strategic acquisitions.&#160; Prior to SGS, Mr. Bartusek was Director of Finance for the North American operations at XEROX Global Services, Inc.&#160; Mr. Bartusek also held various financial positions at GE Capital and was a Senior Manager at PricewaterhouseCoopers LLP<font style="FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#160;</font>where he gained experience in the assurance and business advisory group working with large public companies as well as emerging technology organizations.&#160; Mr. Bartusek graduated from Edinboro University with a B.S. in Business Administration, is a Certified Public Accountant, and holds a Green Belt Six Sigma certification.</div>

<div><br>
</div>

<div style="TEXT-ALIGN: justify; FONT-FAMILY: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; FONT-SIZE: 10pt">&#8220;We are pleased to welcome Mike to our Company,&#8221; said Ronald Casciano, CEO &amp; President of PAR Technology. &#8220;Mike has held several challenging financial leadership roles and has excelled in many diverse areas.&#160; He is distinctly qualified to serve as PAR&#8217;s CFO and we are looking forward to leveraging his expertise for both the day-to-day financial operations of the Company and long-term strategic planning.&#8221;</div>
