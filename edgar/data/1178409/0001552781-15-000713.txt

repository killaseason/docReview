<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><B>Tidelands Bancshares Announces Quarterly Results</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">Tidelands Bancshares, Inc., the holding company for Tidelands Bank,
announced that for the three months ended June 30, 2015, the net income before loan loss provision expense was $35,565 as compared
to $60,886 for the same period of 2014. After providing $1.2 million to the allowance for loan loss, the second quarter net loss
was $1.1 million as compared to net income of $30,886 for the same period in 2014. The additional provision expense allowed us
to resolve several significant non-performing loans and increase the allowance to 1.7% of outstanding loans from a level of 1.5%
at December 31, 2014. After preferred stock dividend accruals for the three months ended June 30, 2015, the loss available to common
shareholders was $1.6 million as compared to a net loss of $355,945 for the same period of 2014.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">The Balance Sheet showed positive improvements. Gross Loans were
$319.3 million or an increase of $1.2 million. Non-Interest bearing checking is up $3.7 million, Savings and Money Market is up
$9.2 million and Time deposits are down $6.9 million from December 31, 2014. This is evidence of further improvement in the makeup
of our deposit customer base. We were also able to move balances into transaction based deposits and away from more expensive time
deposits. Other Real Estate was $13.5 million at June 30, 2015 representing a $4.0 million decline from $17.5 million at December
31, 2014. Loan quality showed continued improvement as total substandard loans were $14.6 million at June 30, 2015 down 51.3% from
the $30.4 million at December 31, 2014.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><B>About Tidelands Bancshares, Inc.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0"><B>&nbsp;</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0">Tidelands Bancshares, Inc., headquartered in Mt. Pleasant, SC, operates
as a bank holding company of Tidelands Bank. Tidelands Bank is a local community bank focused on serving individuals, families,
entrepreneurs, and small businesses in the South Carolina Lowcountry, with 7 locations serving Charleston, Dorchester, Berkeley,
Horry, and Beaufort counties. Tidelands Bank offers mortgages, construction loans, deposit products, internet banking, 24 hour
telephone banking, and ATM service, and takes great pride in providing the custom banking solutions and services necessary to meet
customer needs. Traded on the OTC Pink market as <B>TDBK,</B> Tidelands can also be found on the web at www.tidelandsbank.com.
For more information regarding the matters described in this press release, please refer to Tidelands Bancshares, Inc.&rsquo;s
filings, including on Form 10-K, with the Securities and Exchange Commission at www.sec.gov.</P>
