 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Item&nbsp;5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain
Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers. </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">On July&nbsp;23, 2015, the Board of Directors of First Interstate BancSystem, Inc. (the
&#147;Company&#148;), approved the appointment of Kevin P. Riley to serve as President and Chief Executive Officer of the Company effective September&nbsp;23, 2015. Mr.&nbsp;Riley will replace Ed Garding, who is retiring as the Company&#146;s
President and Chief Executive Officer on September&nbsp;23, 2015. To ensure a smooth transition, Mr.&nbsp;Garding will continue his employment with the Company through January&nbsp;29, 2016, working closely with Mr.&nbsp;Riley and assisting with
special projects. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Riley, 55, has served as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of the Company since August 2013. Mr.&nbsp;Riley
has also served as a director of First Interstate Bank, the Company&#146;s banking subsidiary, since August 2013. Prior to his employment with the Company, Mr.&nbsp;Riley served as executive vice president and chief financial officer of Berkshire
Hills Bancorp in Massachusetts since 2007, and in various executive level positions with KeyCorp since 1986. Mr.&nbsp;Riley earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration for Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts and is
a certified public accountant. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Also on July&nbsp;23, 2015, the Company&#146;s Board of Directors approved the appointment of Marcy D. Mutch to serve as
Chief Financial Officer of the Company effective September&nbsp;23, 2015. Ms.&nbsp;Mutch, 56, has served as a Senior Vice President of the Company since December 2014 and Vice President of Corporate Tax, with various other duties, since joining the
Company in October 2006. Ms.&nbsp;Mutch has also served as the Company&#146;s Investor Relations Officer since the Company became publicly traded in March 2010. Prior to her employment with the Company, Ms.&nbsp;Mutch served in tax and finance
positions with Citizens Development Company, a&nbsp;bank holding company, from 2000 to 2006, and as tax manager for Eide Bailly LLP from 1981 to mid-2006. Ms.&nbsp;Mutch received her Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from Montana
State University-Billings in Billings, Montana and is a certified public accountant. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">A copy of the press release announcing these appointments is
furnished herewith as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated by reference herein. </P> <P STYLE="font-size:18pt;margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt">&nbsp;</P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="right"><B>Exhibit 99.1 </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>First Interstate BancSystem, Inc. Announces CEO Succession </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Company Release: July&nbsp;23, 2015 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">First Interstate
BancSystem, Inc.&#146;s (Nasdaq: FIBK) Board of Directors, at a meeting held on July&nbsp;23, 2015, approved the appointment of Kevin P. Riley as President and CEO, effective September&nbsp;23, 2015. Mr.&nbsp;Riley will replace President and CEO
Edward Garding, who is retiring effective September&nbsp;23, 2015, after 44 years with First Interstate BancSystem. To ensure a smooth transition, Mr.&nbsp;Garding will continue his employment with the Company through January&nbsp;29, 2016, working
closely with Mr.&nbsp;Riley and assisting with special projects. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Executive Vice Chair of the Board, Jim Scott, recognized Mr.&nbsp;Garding&#146;s many
years of service and leadership. &#147;From his first days as a management trainee to his last three years as President and CEO, Ed has always had a fierce commitment to our corporate values, supporting our employees and the communities they serve.
On behalf of the Board, management and employees, I sincerely thank Ed for all he has done in service for our Company and our customers,&#148; stated Mr.&nbsp;Scott. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;I have enjoyed being a part of this amazing organization. Now, I am looking forward to the next phase of my life, enjoying more time with my wife, four
children and two grandchildren,&#148; said Mr.&nbsp;Garding. &#147;First Interstate has more than 1,700 talented employees and a supportive board. With their dedication and Kevin&#146;s leadership, I am confident we will continue to grow and
prosper.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Riley has nearly 30 years of experience in many different areas of the financial services industry, including management of
finance, investments, customer relationship management, bank operations, information technology, data governance, and sales. &#147;Kevin brings relevant leadership experience and a positive energy that aligns very well with the strategic direction
of First Interstate,&#148; said Mr.&nbsp;Scott. &#147;This gives him the knowledge, perspective and expertise to lead our company into the future.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Riley has served as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of First Interstate since August 2013. During this time, he also served as a
director of the Company&#146;s banking subsidiary. Prior to his employment with First Interstate, Mr.&nbsp;Riley served as Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer of Berkshire Hills Bancorp in Massachusetts since 2007, and in various
executive level positions with KeyCorp since 1986. He earned his Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from Northeastern University in Boston, Massachusetts and is a certified public accountant. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Riley said he is honored to be asked to succeed Mr.&nbsp;Garding as President and CEO. He further stated, &#147;First Interstate plays such a big
role in our communities and in the lives of our customers. This commitment comes from loyal employees who are dedicated to serving our customers and helping them meet their financial goals. I look forward to building on this strong foundation and
working with our employees and leadership team towards continued growth and success.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Mr.&nbsp;Riley and his wife Laurie have four adult children,
three of whom are now living in Montana. &#147;My father has lived in Cody, Wyoming for the past 20 years. I have been a frequent visitor over these years and have learned a great deal about this region, its economy and its communities. My family
and I are blessed to live and work here,&#148; said Mr.&nbsp;Riley. </P>
