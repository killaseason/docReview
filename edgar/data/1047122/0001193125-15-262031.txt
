<P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Robert Beauchamp elected to Raytheon Board of Directors </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">WALTHAM, Mass. (July 24, 2015) &#150; The Board of Directors of Raytheon Company (NYSE: RTN) has elected Robert &#147;Bob&#148; Beauchamp as a director,
effective immediately. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Beauchamp, 55, is chairman, president and CEO of BMC Software, Inc., a global provider of business service management software. He
joined BMC in 1988 and has served as president and CEO since 2001. Beauchamp was named board chairman of BMC in 2008. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#147;Bob&#146;s extensive
leadership experience and deep understanding of today&#146;s digital technology markets strongly align with the business focal points of our Board and our Company,&#148; said Thomas A. Kennedy, Raytheon Company Chairman and CEO. &#147;We look
forward to leveraging Bob&#146;s expertise on the Board as we continue to execute our long-term, global growth strategy.&#148; </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Beauchamp serves as an
