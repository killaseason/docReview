<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">ISELIN, NJ July 23,
2015 -- Middlesex Water Company (NASDAQ: MSEX) announced today that its Board of Directors has approved a regular quarterly dividend
of $0.1925 per common share payable on September 1, 2015 to holders of record as of August 14, 2015. Middlesex Water has paid cash
dividends in varying amounts continually since 1912 and has increased its dividend for 42 consecutive years.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in; background-color: white">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0; text-indent: 0.5in">Organized in 1897, Middlesex Water provides
regulated and unregulated water and wastewater utility services in New Jersey, Delaware and Pennsylvania through various subsidiary
companies. To learn more about Middlesex Water, including information about its Dividend Reinvestment and Common Stock Purchase
Plan, visit the Investor Relations section at <U>www.middlesexwater.com</U> or call (732) 638-7549.</P>
