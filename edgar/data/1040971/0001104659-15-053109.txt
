<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 1.01</font></b><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font><b>Entry into a Material Definitive Agreement.</b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Nineteenth Amendment to the First Amended and Restated Agreement of Limited Partnership of SL Green Operating Partnership, L.P.</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On July&nbsp;22, 2015, SL Green Realty Corp. (the &#147;Company&#148;), as the general partner of SL Green Operating Partnership, L.P. (the &#147;Operating Partnership&#148;), entered into a nineteenth amendment (the &#147;Nineteenth Amendment&#148;) to the Operating Partnership&#146;s First Amended and Restated Agreement of Limited Partnership, dated August&nbsp;20, 1997 (as amended through the date hereof, the &#147;Partnership Agreement&#148;), in respect of the issuance of 200,000 Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units of the Operating Partnership (the &#147;Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units&#148;) of limited partnership interests with a liquidation preference of $25.00 per unit (the &#147;Series&nbsp;P Liquidation Preference&#148;). 200,000 Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units have been issued as a portion of the consideration for the acquisition of ownership interests in a certain commercial real estate property. The Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units are not convertible into or exchangeable for any other property or securities of the Partnership or the Company.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units provide for a cumulative quarterly preferential cash distribution of 4.00% of the Series&nbsp;P Liquidation Preference per annum.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Series&nbsp;P Preferred Units were issued in reliance on the exemption from registration provided by Section&nbsp;4(a)(2)&nbsp;of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the &#147;Securities Act&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The foregoing description of the Nineteenth Amendment is qualified in its entirety by reference to the Nineteenth Amendment, a copy of which is filed herewith as Exhibit&nbsp;10.1 and incorporated herein by reference.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><i><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-style:italic;">Twentieth Amendment to the First Amended and Restated Agreement of Limited Partnership of SL Green Operating Partnership, L.P.</font></i></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On July&nbsp;22, 2015, the Company, as the general partner of the Operating Partnership, entered into a twentieth amendment (the &#147;Twentieth Amendment&#148;) to the Partnership Agreement, in respect of the issuance of 268,000 Series&nbsp;Q Convertible Preferred Units of the Operating Partnership (the &#147;Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units&#148;) of limited partnership interests with a liquidation preference of $25.00 per unit (the &#147;Series&nbsp;Q Liquidation Preference&#148;). 268,000 Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units have been issued as a portion of the consideration for the acquisition of ownership interests in a certain commercial real estate property.&nbsp; The terms of the Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units provide, among other things, that the Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units may be converted into common units of the Operating Partnership (&#147;Common Units&#148;), and following such conversion, in certain circumstances may be redeemed for shares of the Company&#146;s common stock, par value $0.01 per share (&#147;Common Stock&#148;).&nbsp; The initial conversion price of Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units is $148.95 per Common Unit.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units provide for a cumulative quarterly preferential cash distribution of 3.50% of the Series&nbsp;Q Liquidation Preference per annum.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">The Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units were issued in reliance on the exemption from registration provided by Section&nbsp;4(a)(2)&nbsp;of the Securities Act.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">WHEREAS, pursuant to a Purchase, Sale, Exchange and Contribution Agreement (the &#147;<u>Contribution Agreement</u>&#148;), dated as of June&nbsp;16, 2015, by and among an affiliate of the Partnership (&#147;<u>Member</u>&#148;), the unit recipient (the &#147;<u>Transferor</u>&#148;) and the other parties party thereto, the Transferor transferred its interests in an entity that owned a commercial real estate property to Member in exchange for the applicable consideration specified in the Contribution Agreement, which includes the Series&nbsp;Q Preferred Units (as defined herein).</font></p>

<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
