<hr style="margin-bottom: 9.6px; padding-top: 9.6px;" noshade size="1"><p style="margin:0px; padding-left:66px; text-indent:-66px; page-break-before:always"><b>Item 5.02 &nbsp;&nbsp;Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.</b></p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">On July 21, 2015, the Board of Directors (the <font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&ldquo;</font>Board<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rdquo;</font>) of Everyday Health, Inc. (the <font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&ldquo;</font>Company<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rdquo;</font>) appointed Laizer Kornwasser as a director of the Company, effective as of July 22, 2015.</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">Mr. Kornwasser was designated as a Class I director and shall serve for the remainder of the current Class I term, which expires at the annual meeting of stockholders to be held in 2018. &nbsp;The size of the Board was increased to eight members to accommodate Mr. Kornwasser<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rsquo;</font>s appointment. &nbsp;Mr. Kornwasser will join the Audit Committee of the Board (the <font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&ldquo;</font>Committee<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rdquo;</font>), effective as of August 15, 2015. &nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">There is no arrangement or understanding with any person pursuant to which Mr. Kornwasser was appointed as a member of the Board.</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">Mr. Kornwasser will receive compensation for his services in accordance with the Company<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rsquo;</font>s Non-Employee Director Compensation Policy, under which he will receive a pro-rated annual retainer of $30,000 and $10,000 for his service on the Board and the Committee, respectively. &nbsp;In addition, Mr. Kornwasser will receive a one-time equity grant having an estimated fair value on the date of grant of $150,000. </p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">The Company intends to enter into its standard form of indemnification agreement with Mr. Kornwasser, the form of which was previously filed by the Company as Exhibit 10.4 to the Company<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Times New Roman">&rsquo;</font>s Registration Statement on Form S-1 (File No. 333-194097), originally filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on February 24, 2014, as amended.</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">Mr. Kornwasser recently served as Executive Vice President, Company Group Chairman for Valeant Pharmaceuticals International, Inc. While at Valeant Pharmaceuticals, from February 2013 until June 2015, Mr. Kornwasser was responsible for the neurology, dental, aesthetics, generics, managed care and trade divisions, as well as the Canadian operations. Previously, Mr. Kornwasser worked in various positions at Medco Health Solutions, Inc. from August 2003 until its acquisition by Express Scripts in April 2012, most recently serving as Senior Vice President of Consumer Solutions and Retail Markets. Prior to Medco Health Solutions, Mr. Kornwasser held positions at Opera Solutions, Walker Digital, Merrill Lynch and Coopers &amp; Lybrand and served as an Associate Professor at Yeshiva University. &nbsp;Mr. Kornwasser received a B.S. in Accounting from Yeshiva University and an M.B.A from Harvard Business School.</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; text-indent:48px">The press release announcing the appointment of Mr. Kornwasser to the Board is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K. &nbsp;</p>

<p style="margin:0px; font-family:Arial; font-size:11pt" align="center"><b>Everyday Health, Inc. Appoints Laizer Kornwasser to the Board of Directors</b></p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; font-family:Arial">NEW YORK, July 23, 2015 <font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&ndash;</font>&nbsp;Everyday Health, Inc. (NYSE: EVDY), a leading digital health and wellness company, today announced that Laizer D. Kornwasser will join its board of directors effective immediately. &nbsp;Mr. Kornwasser has deep experience across the healthcare industry, having held senior executive positions at Valeant Pharmaceuticals International and Medco Health Solutions. &nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; font-family:Arial"><font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&ldquo;</font>We are extremely excited for Laizer to be joining our board of directors,<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&rdquo;</font> said Ben Wolin, co-founder and CEO of Everyday Health. <font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&ldquo;</font>His knowledge of the dramatic changes occurring across the healthcare landscape is unparalleled, and he has achieved great success navigating these changes. Laizer<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&rsquo;</font>s deep understanding of the business goals across pharmaceutical companies, payors and providers, as well as his success in driving strategic M&amp;A, will be beneficial as we continue to scale our business and innovate across the healthcare sector.<font style="font-family:Arial Unicode MS,Arial">&rdquo;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; font-family:Arial">Mr. Kornwasser has over twenty years of experience in the healthcare, financial services and technology sectors. &nbsp;Most recently, Mr. Kornwasser served as Executive Vice President, Company Group Chairman for Valeant Pharmaceuticals International, Inc. At Valeant Pharmaceuticals, Mr. Kornwasser was responsible for the neurology, dental, aesthetics, generics, managed care and trade divisions, as well as the Canadian operations. Previously, Mr. Kornwasser held senior executive roles at Medco Health Solutions, Inc. from August 2003 until its acquisition by Express Scripts in April 2012, including Senior Vice President of Consumer Solutions and Retail Markets. Prior to joining Medco, Mr. Kornwasser held positions at Merrill Lynch, Coopers &amp; Lybrand, Opera Solutions and Walker Digital and served as an associate professor at Yeshiva University. &nbsp;He has an M.B.A. from Harvard Business School and a B.S. in Accounting from Yeshiva University. </p>
<p style="margin:0px"><br></p>
<p style="margin:0px; font-family:Arial"><b>About Everyday Health, Inc.</b> Everyday Health, Inc. (NYSE: EVDY) is a leading provider of digital health and wellness solutions. Everyday Health attracts a large and engaged audience of consumers and healthcare professionals to its premier health and wellness properties, and utilizes its data and analytics expertise to deliver highly personalized content experiences and efficient and effective marketing and engagement solutions. Everyday Health enables consumers to manage their daily health and wellness needs, healthcare professionals to stay informed and make better decisions for their patients, and marketers, health payors and providers to communicate and engage with consumers and healthcare professionals to drive better health outcomes. Everyday Health's content and solutions are delivered through multiple channels, including desktop, mobile web, and mobile phone and tablet applications, as well as video and social media.</p>
