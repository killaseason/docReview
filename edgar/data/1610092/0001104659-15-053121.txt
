<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt .75in;text-indent:-.75in;"><b><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;font-weight:bold;">Item 1.02</font></b>&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; <b>Termination of a Material Definitive Agreement. </b></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On July&nbsp;21, 2015, Philip R. Hagerman, the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of Diplomat Pharmacy,&nbsp;Inc., a Michigan corporation (the &#147;Company&#148;), executed a written notice terminating the Voting Agreement, dated as of August&nbsp;15, 2014 (the &#147;Voting Agreement&#148;), by and among the Company, Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman, certain members of Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman&#146;s immediate family and various trusts affiliated with or for the benefit of such persons, Jeffrey Rowe, and certain trusts controlled by Mr.&nbsp;Rowe or his family members.&#160; Mr.&nbsp;Rowe is Executive Vice President-Operations and a member of the Company&#146;s Board of Directors.&#160; The Voting Agreement was terminated in the sole discretion of Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman pursuant to Section&nbsp;9(c)&nbsp;thereof and was immediately effective.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;text-indent:.5in;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Under the terms of the Voting Agreement, the parties had agreed to vote their shares of common stock based on the voting determination of a majority of the shares held by such voting group, effectively giving voting control over such shares to Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman.&#160; Immediately prior to such termination, Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman beneficially owned approximately 41% of the Company&#146;s common stock.&#160; Immediately following such termination, Mr.&nbsp;Hagerman and Mr.&nbsp;Rowe beneficially owned approximately 36% and 3%, respectively, of the Company&#146;s common stock.</font></p>
