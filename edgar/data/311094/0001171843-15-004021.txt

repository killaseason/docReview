<html><head><title>Westamerica Bancorporation Declares Quarterly Dividend and Announces Stock Repurchase Plan</title></head><body><p align="right">EXHIBIT 99.1</p><h3 align="center">Westamerica Bancorporation Declares Quarterly Dividend and Announces Stock Repurchase Plan</h3><p>SAN RAFAEL, Calif., July 23, 2015 (GLOBE NEWSWIRE) -- The Board of Directors of Westamerica Bancorporation (NASDAQ:WABC) today declared a quarterly cash dividend of $0.38 per share on common stock outstanding to shareholders of record at the close of business August 3, 2015<strong>. </strong>The dividend is payable August 14, 2015.</p><p>
	Westamerica Bancorporation's Board of Directors also approved a plan to repurchase, as conditions warrant, up to 1,750,000 shares of the Company's common stock on the open market or in privately negotiated transactions prior to September 1, 2016. The repurchase plan represents approximately 7 percent of the Company's common stock outstanding as of June 30, 2015.</p><p>
	Chairman, President and CEO David Payne stated, "This dividend and share repurchase program recognize Westamerica's relatively high-level of profitability, healthy capital level, and stable asset quality."</p><p>
	On July 15, 2015, Westamerica reported $14.8 million in net income for the three months ended June 30, 2015, or $0.58 diluted earnings per common share.</p><p>
	Westamerica Bancorporation, through its wholly owned subsidiary, Westamerica Bank, operates banking and trust offices throughout Northern and Central California.</p><p style="text-align: center">
	<em>Westamerica Bancorporation Web Address: www.westamerica.com</em></p><p>
