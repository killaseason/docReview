<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>New York, New York, July 23, 2015 &ndash;
</B>Global Net Lease, Inc. (NYSE: GNL) (&ldquo;GNL&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;), announced today it will release its financial
results for the second quarter 2015 on Monday, August 10, 2015, before the market open. The Company will host an earnings conference
call reviewing these results and its operations the same day, beginning at 10:00 a.m. ET. The call will be conducted by Scott Bowman,
the Company&rsquo;s Chief Executive Officer, Patrick Goulding, the Company&rsquo;s Chief Financial Officer, Andrew Winer, the Company&rsquo;s
President and Chief Investment Officer, and Andrew Backman, Managing Director, Investor Relations and Public Relations for GNL.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Dial-in instructions for the conference
call and the replay are outlined below. This conference call will also be broadcast live over the Internet and can be accessed
by all interested parties through the GNL website, <FONT STYLE="color: Blue"><U>www.globalnetlease.com</U></FONT>, in the &ldquo;Investor
Relations&rdquo; section. To listen to the live call, please go to the &ldquo;Investor Relations&rdquo; section of the Company&rsquo;s
website at least 15 minutes prior to the start of the call to register and download any necessary audio software. For those who
are not able to listen to the live broadcast, a replay will be available shortly after the call on the GNL website.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify"><B>About Global Net Lease, Inc.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Global Net Lease, Inc. (NYSE: GNL) is

with an emphasis on sale-leaseback transactions involving single tenant, mission critical income producing net-leased assets across
