<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(b)&nbsp; Resignation of Director</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Seiji Inagaki, a director of the Board of Directors (&#147;Board&#148;) of Janus Capital Group Inc. (&#147;Company&#148;) informed the Board on July&nbsp;23, 2015, that he is resigning from the Board effective July&nbsp;24, 2015. Mr.&nbsp;Inagaki was appointed to the Board in January&nbsp;2013 as a designee by The Dai-ichi Life Insurance Company, Limited (&#147;Dai-ichi Life&#148;) pursuant to the Investment and Strategic Cooperation Agreement between the Company and Dai-ichi Life dated as of August&nbsp;10, 2012 (the &#147;Agreement&#148;).</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="font-size:10.0pt;margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">(d)</font><font size="1" style="font-size:3.0pt;">&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160;&#160; </font>Election of Directors</p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">On July&nbsp;24, 2015, the Board appointed Tatsusaburo Yamamoto to fill the vacancy created by Mr.&nbsp;Inagaki&#146;s resignation. Mr.&nbsp;Yamamoto was designated for nomination by Dai-ichi Life pursuant to the Agreement, which grants Dai-ichi Life the right to designate a representative for appointment to the Company&#146;s Board as long as Dai-ichi Life maintains ownership of at least 15% of the issued and outstanding shares of the Company&#146;s stock or until the right is otherwise terminated in accordance with the terms of the Agreement.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Yamamoto is currently an executive officer and general manager of the Investment Planning Department of Dai-ichi Life.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Prior to his appointment, the Nominating and Corporate Governance Committee of the Board met with Mr.&nbsp;Yamamoto, reviewed his background and business experience and determined that Mr.&nbsp;Yamamoto possessed the membership criteria for non-employee directors as set forth in the Corporate Governance Guidelines.</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">&nbsp;</font></p>
<p style="margin:0in 0in .0001pt;"><font size="2" face="Times New Roman" style="font-size:10.0pt;">Mr.&nbsp;Yamamoto will not receive any compensation for serving as a director and is not expected to serve on any committees of the Board.</font></p>
