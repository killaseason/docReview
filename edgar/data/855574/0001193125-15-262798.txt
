</TABLE> <P STYLE="margin-top:24pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>AMERIANA BANCORP REPORTS SECOND QUARTER 2015 NET INCOME </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>OF $278,000 OR $0.09 PER SHARE </B></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">NEW
CASTLE, Ind. (July 24, 2015) &#150; Ameriana Bancorp (NASDAQ: ASBI), parent company for Ameriana Bank, today announced earnings for the second quarter of 2015 of $278,000, or $0.09 per basic and diluted share, compared with $603,000, or $0.20 per
basic and diluted share, for the second quarter of 2014. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">For the first six months of 2015, Ameriana recorded net income of $780,000, or
$0.26 per basic and diluted share, compared with $1.3 million, or $0.45 per basic and diluted share, in the year-earlier period. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Commenting on the announcement, Jerome J. Gassen, President and Chief Executive Officer, said, &#147;Earnings for the second quarter were down
sharply from the year earlier quarter due to $407,000 in legal and investment banking fees, as well as other costs related to our recently announced merger with Muncie, Indiana-based First Merchants Corporation. We are excited about the opportunity
to join the First Merchants family, and look forward to the Ameriana franchise becoming a part of one of the finest banking organizations in our market area.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">In late 2015, Ameriana Bank will open its first urban banking center in the Broad Ripple Meridian Kessler neighborhoods in Indianapolis. The
Bank&#146;s innovative approach combines high-tech amenities and neighborhood-centric branding and design strategies with the Bank&#146;s continued dedication to deliver best-in-class service and financial expertise. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Gassen also commented that he was &#147;very pleased with the Bank&#146;s continuing success in loan portfolio growth, with an increase of
$7.5 million, or 2.4% in the second quarter, bringing total growth for the first half of 2015 to $11.3 million. Although deposit growth was flat for the second quarter, we are up $10.5 million, or 2.8% for the first half of this year.&#148; He added
that &#147;with our current strong commercial loan pipeline, we believe we are likely to experience additional loan portfolio growth in the third quarter.&#148; </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ameriana&#146;s net interest margin improved six basis points to 3.50% for the second quarter of 2015 compared with the first quarter of 2015,
due primarily to growth in both the loan portfolio and investment securities portfolio that utilized funds previously earning a lower yield in overnight investments. </P>

 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">ASBI Reports Second Quarter 2015 Results </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"> Page

 </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">July 24, 2015 </P> <p STYLE="margin-top:0pt;margin-bottom:0pt ; font-size:8pt">&nbsp;</P>
 <P STYLE="margin-top:0pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Total loans receivable of $331.3 million at June&nbsp;30, 2015 represented an increase of
$11.3 million, or 3.5%, from December&nbsp;31, 2014, while total investment securities of $65.0 million represented an increase of $9.8 million from December&nbsp;31, 2014. For the same six-month period, total deposits increased $10.5 million, or
2.8%, to $389.4 million at June&nbsp;30, 2015. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The Bank&#146;s allowance for loan losses was $3.9 million at June&nbsp;30, 2015,
basically unchanged from December&nbsp;31, 2014. The allowance for loan losses at June&nbsp;30, 2015 as a percentage of loans receivable and as a percentage of non-performing loans was 1.18% and 86.0%, respectively. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; text-indent:4%; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Ameriana Bancorp&#146;s reported book value per common share increased $0.12 per share to $13.71 at June&nbsp;30, 2015 from $13.59 at
December&nbsp;31, 2014. Ameriana Bancorp and Ameriana Bank exceeded all regulatory capital standards to be considered &#147;well-capitalized&#148; at June&nbsp;30, 2015. </P>

banking services and provides a range of investments and securities products through banking centers in the central Indiana area. Ameriana Bank owns Ameriana Insurance Agency, a full-service insurance agency, and Ameriana Financial Services, which
